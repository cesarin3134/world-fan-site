FAQ

Why are some matches missing data?

Match data is fetched live from public sources. If a source is temporarily unavailable, some details may be missing until the next refresh.

Reminders are not firing.

Check that notifications are enabled for World Fan in your device Settings. Reminders are scheduled locally; no internet is required to fire them.

How do I reset predictions or favorites?

Uninstalling the app removes all local data. There is no account to delete because no account exists.

Does the app work offline?

Cached data is shown when offline. Live updates require an internet connection.

Which languages are supported?

English, Español (ES/MX), Português (PT), Italiano, Deutsch.
