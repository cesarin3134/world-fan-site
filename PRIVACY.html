Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-27

Summary

World Fan does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on a server. All app data stays on your device.

What we store locally

The following are stored only in your device's local storage (MMKV):

  • Your favorite teams
  • Your match reminders
  • Your match predictions and points
  • Your chosen language
  • Your display name (optional, if you set one)

Uninstalling the app removes all of this.

What we fetch

The app fetches public data from third-party sources to display content:

  • worldcup26.ir — match schedule, scores, scorers, group standings
  • openfootball/worldcup.json (GitHub) — team squads
  • Wikipedia REST API — optional player photos
  • DiceBear (api.dicebear.com) — initials avatars when no photo is available
  • Google News RSS via api.rss2json.com — tournament news

These services may log standard request metadata (IP, timestamp). We have no control over their policies.

Notifications

The app may schedule local notifications for match reminders if you grant permission. Notifications are scheduled and fired locally; no push server is involved.

Calendar

If you ever opt into adding a match to your device calendar, the app uses the standard iOS / Android calendar APIs after requesting permission.

Accounts

There are no accounts. There is no sign-up. There is no login.

Tracking & analytics

None.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not collect data from any user, including children.
