Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-28

Summary

World Fan does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on a server. All app data stays on your device. There are no accounts, no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking SDKs. Applies to both the iOS and Android editions.

What we store locally

The following are stored only in your device's local storage (MMKV on iOS and Android):

  • Your favorite teams and matches
  • Your match reminders
  • Your match predictions and earned points
  • Your chosen language
  • Your display name (optional, only if you set one)
  • Cached images and roster data, to reduce network use

Uninstalling the app removes all of this. You can also clear it via the system Settings → app storage.

What we fetch from the network (read-only)

To show live information, the app issues read-only HTTPS requests to public, publisher-owned endpoints. These services receive your IP address and standard HTTPS headers, the same as any web browser visiting the same page. We pass no personal identifier of any kind.

  • worldcup26.ir — match schedule, scores, scorers, group standings
  • openfootball/worldcup.json (raw.githubusercontent.com) — team squads and authoritative goal data
  • News RSS feeds via api.rss2json.com — tournament news from BBC Sport, The Guardian, Marca, Mundo Deportivo, La Gazzetta dello Sport
  • flagcdn.com — team flags
  • upload.wikimedia.org — tournament logo
  • Article publisher pages — only when an article ships without a thumbnail; read once to extract the og:image meta tag, then cached locally

Each publisher has its own privacy policy that governs the request they receive. We have no control over those policies.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions only when you opt into the feature that uses them. You can decline without losing core functionality:

  • Notifications — only to fire match-kickoff reminders you explicitly schedule. Scheduled locally; no push server is involved.
  • Calendar — only when you add a match to your device calendar. Uses the system calendar API after explicit permission.
  • Network access — required to load live scores, schedule, news, and roster data.

We do not request location, contacts, microphone, camera, photos, or any other sensitive permission.

Accounts

There are no accounts. There is no sign-up. There is no login.

Tracking, analytics, and ads

None. No advertising SDKs, no behavioural analytics, no telemetry.

Children

The app is rated 4+ on iOS and Everyone on Google Play. It does not collect data from any user, including children.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will update this page and bump the "Last updated" date above. The current version is always the source of truth.
